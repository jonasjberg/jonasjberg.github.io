Present

Currently working full-time as a software developer and UNIX system administrator consultant.

I am particularly interested in open source, Linux, DevOps, software testing, configuration management, infrastructure as code, version control systems, security, reverse-engineering, low-level debugging, well established standards and ostensibly "best" practices.

I keep an "engineering mindset" at all times — approaching every specific problem with an open mind and a systematic approach, borne from years of experience. All while balancing trade-offs, cost and future return of investment. I keep clear of hype, buzzwords and "silver bullet" solutions that seem so prevalent in modern IT.

I have spent most of my life doing electronics and IT; from my perspective, the often mentioned "full stack" is far from stack enough. Show me a HEX dump of binary data and I will tell you what we are looking at.

I spend a lot of my free time developing autonameow, a program that "automagically" renames files by extracting, analyzing and ranking all kinds of data related to the file. A subset of my projects is available on my GitHub.
